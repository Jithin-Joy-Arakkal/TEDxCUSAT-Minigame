# TEDxCUSAT-Minigame

## Idea Collector

A simple browser-based game themed around TEDxCUSAT. Collect falling ideas (represented by emojis) by clicking on them. Score points and aim to collect 10 ideas to win!

### How to Play
- Open `index.html` in your web browser.
- Click "Start Game" to begin.
- Click on the falling ideas to collect them.
- Collect 10 ideas to win!
- Click "Restart" to play again.

### Files
- `index.html`: The main HTML file.
- `style.css`: Styles for the game.
- `script.js`: Game logic in JavaScript.

### Requirements
- A modern web browser with JavaScript enabled.

Enjoy spreading ideas at TEDxCUSAT!
